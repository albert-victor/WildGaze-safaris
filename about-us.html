Our Story

More Than a Safari Company — A Way of Life

Tanzania is our home, not just our office. Every route we recommend, every lodge we trust, and every guide on our team lives and breathes this land.

Tanzania is a dream destination in every sense — home to unforgettable wildlife and extraordinary scenic beauty. We travel the length and breadth of every destination we recommend to ensure our advice is grounded in firsthand experience, and every supplier in your itinerary is tried and tested.

We are one of Tanzania's most experienced safari operators, offering an exceptional experience at unbeatable value. Mountain climbing and safari are our priorities — we invest significantly in safe, reliable equipment and the best-trained staff in the field. We do not cut corners on safety or service.

Wild Gaze Safaris is owned and operated by Ernest Lugalla and a team of professionals who average more than ten years of experience in safaris and mountain climbing. Our Iringa office serves southern and central Tanzania, with pickups available from Dodoma, Udzungwa, Mikumi, and Dar es Salaam.

Community Commitment 10% of every safari quoted is returned to local communities through clean drinking water, school sponsorships, and sports programmes via the Wild Gaze Charity Foundation.
Verified By Travellers Our guests have reviewed us on TripAdvisor and Safari Bookings — two of the most trusted platforms for African safari planning.
Based in Iringa Our office in Iringa covers the south and central corridor, including Ruaha, Udzungwa, Mikumi, and Nyerere — destinations we know better than anyone.

Ernest Lugalla

Founder · Wild Gaze Safaris Ltd

Every safari we run carries our name — and our reputation. I personally oversee operations because your trust is everything to us.

Ernest founded Wild Gaze Safaris with a simple belief: travellers deserve authentic, expertly guided experiences at honest prices. With over a decade in Tanzania's safari and trekking industry, he leads a team of seasoned professionals from the company's base in Iringa — the gateway to Ruaha, Mikumi, and the southern circuit.

Why Travel With Wild Gaze

Four pillars that define every journey we craft — tap to explore each one.

Every Departure Guaranteed

Once your deposit is received, your safari operates on the scheduled date as described — no cancellations, no supplements for small groups.

  • Fixed departure dates honoured
  • No hidden group-size surcharges
  • Transparent pricing from day one

The Best Guides in the Field

Full-time guides with a minimum of ten years' experience, encyclopaedic knowledge of East Africa's wildlife, and a maximum of four to six guests per vehicle.

  • 10+ years average field experience

Personal Safari Service

Our directors monitor day-to-day operations personally. Staff meet every guest at the airport, assist with luggage, and provide a full pre-safari briefing.
