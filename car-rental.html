Car Rental Tanzania

Whether you need a vehicle for a city transfer, a cross-country road trip, or a full safari circuit, Wild Gaze Safaris offers reliable, well-maintained cars and 4WD vehicles for hire across Tanzania. All rentals include comprehensive insurance, and every vehicle is serviced before departure.

Our fleet ranges from comfortable saloon cars for town use to fully equipped Land Cruiser 4x4s with rooftop tents for independent safari travel. Chauffeured hire with an experienced driver-guide is available for all vehicle types — ideal for airport transfers, business travel, or multi-day itineraries.

We are based in Iringa and cover Dar es Salaam, Dodoma, Arusha, and the southern parks corridor. One-way rentals between cities can be arranged on request.
