Where You Stay

Safari Accommodation

For over thirty years, Wild Gaze Safaris has been handpicking the best lodges, most scenic camps, and most extravagant resorts across Tanzania. Let the guide below help you choose the right accommodation for your desired level of comfort.

Basic

For the budget-conscious traveller. Often public camping and/or shared rooms, with meals prepared by a private chef. Superior in both quality and location.

Comfort

The perfect balance of comfort and price. Small lodges or tented camps with ensuite bathroom facilities.

Comfort Plus

Meets and exceeds the standards of any major international resort. State-of-the-art lodges or tent camps with exquisite service.

Luxury

The most exclusive style available. Private extravagant quarters in the best locations, with select drinks included with meals.
