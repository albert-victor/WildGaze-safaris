Tanzania Safaris

Africa's Greatest Wildlife Destination

Tanzania is home to some of Africa's most iconic landscapes and wildlife experiences — from the open savanna of the Serengeti, the ancient crater of Ngorongoro, and the lakeshore forests of Manyara, to the remote southern wilderness of Ruaha and the Selous Game Reserve, one of the largest protected areas in Africa.

We organise safaris across both the Northern and Southern circuits, tailored to your schedule and interests. Whether you seek the Great Migration, the Big Five, wild dogs and hippos in the south, or a combination of both — our experienced guides ensure you see Tanzania's wildlife in optimum conditions.

Wild Gaze Safaris offers both camping safaris and lodge safaris, with fully inclusive pricing, guaranteed departures, and expert guides based in the southern heartland of Iringa.

◈
Northern Circuit · The Classic

Serengeti, Ngorongoro Crater, Lake Manyara, and Tarangire — the most celebrated safari destinations in Africa, offering the Big Five and the annual wildebeest migration.

◉
Southern Circuit · The Wild

Ruaha, Selous (Nyerere), and Mikumi — Tanzania's remote south, known for larger lion prides, African wild dogs, massive elephant herds, and far fewer crowds.

★
Camping & Lodge · Your Choice

Budget-friendly tented camping with all equipment provided, or handpicked lodges and luxury tented camps — all-inclusive with no hidden costs or supplements.
