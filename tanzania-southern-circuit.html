Southern Circuit Route

Discover Tanzania’s most remote and unspoiled safari wilderness, where vast landscapes, extraordinary wildlife encounters, and authentic adventure combine to create an unforgettable journey through Ruaha, Nyerere, and Mikumi National Parks. Experience thrilling game drives, scenic boat safaris, guided walking adventures, and remarkable opportunities to observe large populations of elephants, lions, wild dogs, and countless bird species across some of East Africa’s most spectacular and least-visited protected areas.

Vast, untamed, and far less crowded – the Southern Circuit offers deep wilderness safaris, river systems, and raw African landscapes.

Nyerere National Park

30,893km² Area
400+Bird Species
Big 5Present

Formerly Selous Game Reserve, Nyerere is one of Africa’s largest protected wilderness areas. Defined by the Rufiji River system, it offers rare boat safaris alongside classic game drives. Elephants, wild dogs, crocodiles, hippos, and lions thrive in this untouched ecosystem.

Elephant Wild Dog Hippo Crocodile Lion
Best: Jun–Oct – wildlife concentrates near rivers
02
Wild Predator Capital
Best: Jun – Nov
Big Cats · Remote Wilderness

Ruaha National Park

Tanzania’s largest national park after Nyerere, Ruaha is a rugged wilderness of baobab valleys and rocky escarpments. It is famous for large lion prides, strong leopard populations, and rare African wild dogs. The Great Ruaha River is the lifeline of this dramatic ecosystem.

Lion Leopard Wild Dog Elephant Kudu
Best: Jun–Nov – peak predator viewing
03
Serengeti Feel
Best: Jun – Oct
Easy Access · Open Plains

Mikumi National Park

Often compared to the Serengeti, Mikumi features wide floodplains filled with elephants, giraffes, zebras, and lions. It is one of the most accessible parks from Dar es Salaam.

Elephant Lion Giraffe Zebra
Best: Jun–Oct
04
Rainforest Trekking
Best: Jun – Mar
Hiking · Endemic Species

Udzungwa Mountains

A biodiversity hotspot with dense rainforest, waterfalls, and rare primates found nowhere else on Earth. Perfect for hiking and nature trekking.

Colobus Monkey Birding Waterfalls Forest Trek
Best: Year-round
